Mission

The focus of the lab is on developing and applying machine learning and high throughput experiment methods to understand the gene regulation and non-coding genetic variants. Specifically, we would like to extract genetic and multi-dimensional epigenetic information from circulating cell-free DNA, exosomes and its related cell types to explore the role of epigenetics in gene regulation and therefore bridge the gap between genetic and phenotypic variations at different healthy and pathological conditions.
